Welcome to Afghan Proverbs


A proverb (from Latin: proverbium) or an adage is a simple, traditional saying that expresses a perceived truth based on common sense or experience. Proverbs are often metaphorical and are an example of formulaic language.







بدون رنج گنج میسر نشود، مزد آن گرفت جان برادر که کار کرد



Treasure cannot be obtained without suffering. The reward for it was the life of a brother who worked.


ژبه هم کلا ده هم بلا

Language is both a fortress and a fortress.


زبان سرخ سر سبز به باد دهد

Let the red-headed green tongue be blown away.


پرسیدن عیب نیست ندانستن عیب است

There is no harm in asking, but there is harm in not knowing.
